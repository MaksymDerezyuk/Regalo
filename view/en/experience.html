    Intern at Comdata

    (June 2022-December 2022)

    For approximately 5-6 months I was an intern at the Comdata Group headquarters in Viladecans (Part of my FP). My main tasks were managing equipment, such as adding more RAM, troubleshooting and repairing it. I was also an AC administrator, where I was in charge of creating new users, resetting passwords or unsubscribing another user. These two tasks were my main tasks although a large part was spent doing the inventory, writing down the names of each equipment and its location and even writing down the number of peripherals we had.


    Being at Comdata was a very important part of my life and even professional development, since that is where I experienced for the first time what the world of work really was and I have been very lucky to be able to find such a good internship tutor (Jesus Garcia Garcia) and some very kind co-workers who were always willing to help me and answer all my questions.
